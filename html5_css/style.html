Legion playstyle

The Dark Angels, like any other space marine legion, they are a good army for new players because space marines are a very flexible army with having tools for every situation and use everything they have for the most part. what separates the Dark Angels from other legions is that when something bad happens to a unit they get stronger with their special rules like Grim Resolve: Dark Angels units retain their Objective Control characteristic while Battle-shocked. and most of their rules and stratagems shows that Dark Angels get stronger if the unit is battle-shocked.
