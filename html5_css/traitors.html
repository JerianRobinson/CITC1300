The Fallen

The Fallen are renegade Space Marines that split of from the Dark Angels ten thousand years ago and have since been scatted across space and time by warp storms. They are the secret enemy of the Unforgiven, a group containing the modern Dark Angels Chapter and its successors. To those Fallen that dwell within the 41st Millennium, many have become Chaos Space Marines, others pirate and renegade warlords, while some have since regretted their heresy and live a life of peaceful seclusion. Others have even gone on to loyally serve the Imperium.

Fallen goals

There are several different types of Fallen. They have no unified outlook or goal. Many Fallen are non-Chaos renegades who seek the destruction of the Imperium in a continuation of their rebellion. Others serve the Chaos Gods and have fully succumbed to Chaos, yet still others realise they were mislead in their rebellion and once again fight in the Emperor's name.
